Heroes Restoration Inc.

Our certified emergency fire damage removal experts use a proprietary 10-step approach to eliminate smoke and soot along with any excess water and dry chemicals from your indoor environment to restore your property back to normal. Our goal is to preserve and restore your interior by following CDC, EPA, and ANSI/IICRC guidelines. It’s important to act quickly to stabilize your fire damaged environment in order to minimize further damages from elements and exposure. We provide thorough water removal and fast acting drying treatments along with smoke damage removal and deodorization making your property safe and toxin free. In addition we provide complete construction, board-up, and demolition as part of our 24-hour emergency fire damage restoration services. Dedicated to our clients, we help you work through the insurance process to aid in restoring your property as quickly as possible. So there is less disruption to you, our IICRC certified technicians utilize 3D imaging to help show underlying damages from fire or water allowing us to remove only what we have to in order to safely restore your property.
